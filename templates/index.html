About this project

Our main aim at xiang-su is to provide people services regarding images and videos, like sometimes we need images of any object in large amount such as 100, then we need to go and google search it but here at xiang-su we have made that task simple, you just tell the name of the object and tell us how many images you need and we will send it to you.

Among other services we provide, one major highlight is that we can convert black and white images and videos to coloured.

We created this website after being inspired by some difficulty we saw some people in our campus were facing, they needed a dataset of images for their project and they were downloading it one by one, from there we got the idea of the image downloader.
